Introduction
About survey This is a webcam survey, you will be asked a series of questions regarding the ease of performing certain facial gestures, and whether these gestures are best suited for certain driving conditions (e.g. is a left eye wink the easiest and most intuitive facial gesture to use to activate a left turn signal, or are two blinks with both eyes in quick succession easier for most users to perform).
Responses to survey questions will be stored in our cloud database, timely linked to facial gestures demonstrated by users.
